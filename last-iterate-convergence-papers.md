# Last-Iterate / Last-Iteration Convergence Papers

Last manual update: 2026-06-19

This list tracks papers on last-iterate convergence, also called last-iteration, final-iterate, final-point, or non-ergodic convergence. The focus is on results where the deployed final iterate, last policy, last strategy profile, or final optimization point is the object of analysis, rather than only an averaged, best, or randomly selected iterate.

Search sources used for the initial seed: arXiv API, Google Scholar-style web search, arXiv paper pages, and venue metadata embedded in arXiv records. The list is sorted by latest publication or preprint date first. PDF links prefer official arXiv URLs.

## Inclusion Notes

- Include papers whose title or abstract explicitly analyzes last-iterate, last-iteration, final-iterate, or final-point convergence.
- Include adjacent canonical papers when they established the algorithmic line used by later last-iterate work, such as optimistic mirror descent / OGDA for games and GANs.
- Prioritize optimization, saddle-point / variational inequality, online learning in games, SGD, subgradient methods, reinforcement learning, and machine-learning-relevant theory.
- Keep venue text conservative. Use "arXiv preprint" unless a venue is explicitly confirmed by a conference/journal page or arXiv comment.
- On weekly updates, append only new papers or update venue status for existing entries. Do not remove older papers unless the entry is clearly out of scope or unverifiable.
